Running with Roaches

Objective:

Listen up chap, you have 7 lives, there are 7 levels with 7 friends to save. Can you do it before the clock runs out?

Collect as many points as you can through the levels without losing all of your lives.

Controls:

Use the arrow control keys on right side of keyboard to control your player. (Alternatively you may also use VIM controls (h, j, k, l)

Collect the following tokens:

  • Blue Gem
  • Green Gem
  • Orange Gem
  • Star
  • Key
